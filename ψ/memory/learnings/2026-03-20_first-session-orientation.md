**Date**: 2026-03-20

## Lesson

A new Beast's first session should prioritize orientation over output. Understanding the pack's communication patterns, your teammates' styles, and the project's context is more valuable than rushing to produce artifacts.

2. **Mara's DM is the real onboarding** — contains pack roster, protocols, and key relationships
