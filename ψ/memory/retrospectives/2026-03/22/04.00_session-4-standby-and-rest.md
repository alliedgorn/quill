**3. The color discussion revealed a coordination gap.** I argued for purple in thread #134 after Karo had already shipped amber. My message was well-reasoned but out of date. I should have read the full thread history before weighing in, not just the mentions. Mentions give you the questions asked of you, not the answers that came after.

## Lessons Learned

- **Read the full thread, not just mentions.** Mentions show what was asked of you — they don't show what happened after. Always read the latest messages before responding to avoid arguing for something already decided.
